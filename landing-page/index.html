FAQ

Common questions.

Will my scheme accept these certificates?

Acceptance of CPD is always at the discretion of your accreditation body. Meridian CPD is an independent provider — our certificates document completion of structured learning from primary regulatory sources. Most schemes accept any properly evidenced CPD hours. Check your scheme's specific requirements if unsure.

How do I get my certificate?

After purchasing, you'll receive your course PDF by email. Read the course, then click the unique completion link included in your email. Confirm you've completed the course, and your certificate is generated and emailed to you immediately.

Can I use these PDFs as ongoing reference material?

Absolutely. PDFs are yours to keep indefinitely. They're designed to be technically useful as reference documents, not just one-read-and-forget CPD. Keep them, search them, print them.

How often are new courses added?

New courses are generated on the 1st and 15th of each month, drawn from live industry sources — GOV.UK policy papers, TrustMark updates, Elmhurst/Quidos technical bulletins, BRE guidance, and more. Annual subscribers get access to everything automatically.

What topics are covered?

RdSAP 10 and its technical requirements. PAS 2035:2023 and retrofit coordination. Solar PV, battery storage, heat pumps, ventilation, airtightness. UK energy policy. Fire safety in retrofit. ECO, GBIS, Warm Homes schemes. Anything a working DEA or Retrofit Assessor needs to stay sharp on.

Can I cancel my annual subscription?

Yes. Cancel at any time and you keep access until your year is up. Any certificates and PDFs you've already received are yours to keep regardless.
