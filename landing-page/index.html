Questions

Will my accreditation body accept these certificates?

Meridian CPD certificates are from an independent provider. Most accreditation schemes (ECMK, Elmhurst, Quidos, Stroma) accept CPD from independent providers provided it is relevant to your practice. We recommend checking your specific scheme's CPD policy. Every certificate is independently verifiable via QR code.

How do I receive my certificate?

After purchasing a course and reading it, click the "I have completed this course" link included in your course delivery email. Your certificate is generated immediately and emailed to you as a PDF.

Can I use the free course code MERIDIAN10?

Yes — apply code MERIDIAN10 at checkout for the RdSAP 10 overview course (MER-DEA-001, normally £19.99) completely free. One use per person.

What format are the courses?

All courses are downloadable PDF files. Save them to your phone, tablet, or computer and read at your own pace. No streaming, no login required after purchase.

How many CPD hours do I need per year?

Most DEA accreditation schemes require 10 CPD hours per year to maintain your licence. Retrofit Assessors typically need 10 hours; Retrofit Coordinators need 25. Our annual subscription at £79 gives you unlimited access to generate as many hours as you need.

When are new courses released?

We publish new courses twice a month, covering emerging changes in RdSAP, PAS 2035, government policy, and sector-specific topics. Subscribers receive an email notification with each new release.
