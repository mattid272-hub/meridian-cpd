1
ICO Registration
Information Commissioner's Office — legal prerequisite · £40/year · 15 minutes

Any UK organisation that processes personal data (learner names, emails, payment records) must register with the ICO. This is a legal requirement under UK GDPR — not optional. It is also a mandatory prerequisite for UKRLP registration.

How to Register

  1. Go to: https://ico.org.uk/registration
  2. Click "Register now"
  3. Work through the self-assessment — select Tier 1 when prompted (small organisation)
  4. Pay £40 by debit or credit card
  5. ICO Registration Number issued by email within 1–3 working days

What You Need

  • Organisation name: Seren Surveying Limited (trading as Meridian CPD)
  • Registered address (Companies House address)
  • Contact email for the business
  • Debit/credit card
What you get: An ICO Registration Number (format: ZA123456). Keep this — it is required for UKRLP registration and demonstrates legal compliance to accreditation bodies.
2
UKRLP Registration
UK Register of Learning Providers · Free · 5 minutes

The UK government's official register of learning providers, managed by the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA). Registration assigns a UKPRN (UK Provider Reference Number) — a unique identifier for Meridian CPD in the government's education data infrastructure. Competitors in the energy assessor sector display this as "Provider No. XXXXXXXX."

How to Register

  1. Go to: https://www.ukrlp.co.uk
  2. Click "Register" or "New Provider Registration"
  3. Complete the 4-step online form — approximately 5 minutes

What You Need

  • Organisation name: Meridian CPD (trading name of Seren Surveying Limited)
  • Registered business address
  • ICO Registration Number (from Step 1)
  • Contact name, phone, and email
  • Brief description of provision — use this text:
"Online CPD courses for UK Domestic Energy Assessors and Retrofit Assessors, covering RdSAP 10, PAS 2035, EPC methodology, and energy efficiency standards. Courses are delivered as structured online modules with assessed completion and certificate issue."
What you get: A UKPRN number displayed on the Meridian CPD website and on learner certificates as "UK Provider Reference Number: XXXXXXXX." Gives Meridian CPD a verified presence in the government's register of learning organisations.
3
CPD Certification Service
cpduk.co.uk · ~£300–700/year · 2 weeks per course · most recognised UK mark

The UK's largest and most widely recognised CPD accreditation body, established 1996. The "CPD Certified" logo is the standard quality mark seen on professional training materials across all sectors in the UK. This is the accreditation that will most influence a DEA's decision to subscribe to Meridian CPD.

No minimum trading history required. Suitable for new providers from day one. No hard pass/fail — they work collaboratively with providers.

How to Apply

  1. Go to: https://www.cpduk.co.uk/become-accredited
  2. Click "Get a Quote" or "Start the Process"
  3. Book a free initial consultation call (30 mins)
  4. They will scope the number of courses, format, and subject matter, then provide a tailored quote
  5. Once agreed and paid, submit course materials for assessment
  6. Assessment: 10–14 working days per course

What to Tell Them on the Call

"Meridian CPD is an online subscription platform providing structured CPD for UK Domestic Energy Assessors and Retrofit Assessors. Courses cover RdSAP 10 updates, EPC methodology, PAS 2035 retrofit coordination, and compliance changes. Each course is approximately 1 hour in duration, delivered via written content with self-assessment questions. Courses are created by qualified practitioners holding MCIOB, RPSA, and PAS 2035 qualifications. We would like to start by accrediting 3 courses."

What to Prepare Before Submission

  • Course title and unique course code
  • Clear learning objectives (3–5 bullet points per course)
  • Structured course content (PDF format)
  • Course duration and delivery format
  • Self-assessment questions (5 per course)
What you get: Licence to display "CPD Certified" logo on all course materials, website, and marketing. Meridian CPD listed in CPD Member Directory. "CPD Certified" symbol on learner certificates. CPD Certified certificate per accredited course.
4
CPD Standards Office
cpdstandards.com · ~£500–1,500/year · ~30 days per course · rigorous dual-accreditation

A respected independent CPD accreditation body founded in 2012, operating in 72 countries. Their trustmark is well-regarded as a more rigorous complement to the CPD Certification Service. Having both marks gives Meridian CPD dual-accreditation that most competitors will not have.

Important: The first membership term requires a minimum 2-year commitment — factor this into budget planning. Payment plans are available. After year two, membership rolls annually.

How to Apply

  1. Go to: https://www.cpdstandards.com/become-accredited
  2. Click "Book a Free Consultation"
  3. Complete a 30-minute call to discuss courses and learning outcomes
  4. Receive a registration form and their "Get CPD Ready" assessment criteria pack
  5. Submit course materials via structured forms they provide
  6. Independent assessor reviews materials over approximately 30 days — collaborative, not pass/fail

What to Tell Them on the Call

Use the same description as Step 3. If Step 3 is already in progress, course materials will already be structured to the right standard — making this step much easier.

What you get: Unique authenticating trustmark (badge with membership number) for website, social media, and documents. CPD Accreditation Certificate per course. Listing in CPD Standards Office Member Directory.
