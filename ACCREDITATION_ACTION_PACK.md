## Step 1 — ICO Registration (prerequisite for Step 2)

**What it is:** The Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) is the UK's data protection regulator. Any organisation that processes personal data (which includes learner names, emails, and payment records) must register. This is a legal requirement — not just a nice-to-have.

**Cost:** £40/year (Tier 1 — small organisation)

**How to do it:**
1. Go to: **https://ico.org.uk/registration**
2. Click **"Register now"**
3. Answer the self-assessment questions — select **Tier 1** when prompted
4. Pay £40 by card
5. You'll receive your **ICO Registration Number** by email within 1–3 working days

**What you'll need:**
- Seren Surveying Limited as the registered organisation name
- Registered address (Companies House address)
- Contact email for the business
- Debit/credit card for payment

**What you get:**
An ICO Registration Number (e.g. `ZA123456`) — required for UKRLP registration and demonstrates legal compliance to accreditation bodies.

## Step 2 — UKRLP Registration (UK Register of Learning Providers)

**What it is:** The UK government's official register of learning providers, managed by the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA). Over 50,000 providers are listed. Registration assigns a **UKPRN** (UK Provider Reference Number) — a unique identifier for Meridian CPD in the government's education infrastructure.

This is the same type of registration that competitor platforms display as "Provider No. XXXXXXXX."

**Cost:** Free (the only cost is the ICO fee from Step 1)

**How to do it:**
1. Go to: **https://www.ukrlp.co.uk**
2. Click **"Register"** or **"New Provider Registration"**
3. Complete the 4-step online form — takes approximately 5 minutes
4. You will need:
   - Organisation name: **Meridian CPD** (trading name of Seren Surveying Limited)
   - Registered address
   - ICO Registration Number (from Step 1)
   - Contact name, phone, and email
   - Brief description of provision: *"Online CPD courses for UK Domestic Energy Assessors and Retrofit Assessors, covering RdSAP, PAS 2035, EPCs, and energy efficiency standards."*

**What you get:**
A **UKPRN number** — displayed on the Meridian CPD website and certificates as "UK Provider Reference Number: XXXXXXXX." Gives Meridian CPD a government-verified presence as a learning organisation.

## Step 3 — CPD Certification Service

**Website:** https://www.cpduk.co.uk
**What it is:** The UK's largest and most widely recognised independent CPD accreditation body, established in 1996. The **"CPD Certified"** logo is the standard quality mark seen on training materials across all professional sectors in the UK. This is the accreditation that will most influence a DEA's decision to subscribe.

**Estimated cost:** £300–£700/year for a small provider (exact figure requires a quote — pricing is bespoke)

**No minimum trading history required. Suitable for new providers from day one.**

**How to do it:**
1. Go to: **https://www.cpduk.co.uk/become-accredited**
2. Click **"Get a Quote"** or **"Start the Process"**
3. Book a free initial consultation call — they will ask about:
   - Number of courses you want to accredit
   - Course format (online, in-person, blended)
   - Subject matter and target audience
   - Approximate duration of each course
4. They will provide a tailored quote based on membership tier
5. Once you agree and pay, you submit course materials for assessment

**What to prepare for the consultation call:**
- Number of courses to accredit (we can start with 2–3 flagship courses)
- Each course has: clear title, learning objectives, structured content, and a duration (e.g. 1 hour)
- Format: online, self-paced, video + quiz

**Assessment timeline:** 10–14 working days per course
**Pass/fail:** They provide guidance rather than a hard fail — collaborative process

**What you get:**
- Licence to display the **"CPD Certified"** logo on all course materials, website, and marketing
- Meridian CPD listed in the **CPD Member Directory** (used by learners searching for courses)
- **CPD Certified certificate** for each accredited course
- "CPD Certified" symbol on certificates issued to learners on completion

## Step 4 — CPD Standards Office

**Website:** https://www.cpdstandards.com
**What it is:** A respected independent CPD accreditation body founded in 2012, operating in 72 countries. Their trustmark is well-regarded as a more rigorous complement to the CPD Certification Service. Having both marks gives Meridian CPD a strong dual-accreditation that most competitors will not have.

**Estimated cost:** £500–£1,500/year for a small provider (exact figure requires a quote)
**Important:** First membership term is a **minimum 2-year commitment** — factor this into budget planning. Payment plans are available.

**How to do it:**
1. Go to: **https://www.cpdstandards.com/become-accredited**
2. Click **"Book a Free Consultation"**
3. The consultation is a 30-minute call to discuss your courses and learning outcomes
4. After the call, you'll receive a registration form and their assessment criteria document ("Get CPD Ready" pack)
5. Submit course materials via structured forms they provide
6. An independent assessor reviews materials over approximately 30 days

**What to prepare:**
- Same course information as Step 3
- If Step 3 is already in progress or complete, this becomes much easier — the course materials will already be structured to the right standard

**What you get:**
- A unique **authenticating trustmark** (badge with your membership number) — for website, social media, course materials, and official documents
- **CPD Accreditation Certificate** for each course
- Listing in the **CPD Standards Office Member Directory**
- "No pass or fail" policy — they work collaboratively to reach the standard
